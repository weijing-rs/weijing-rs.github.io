Biography

I am focusing on remote sensing of atmospheric aerosols, clouds, particulate matter, and trace gases. Up to now, I have authored over 100 papers in leading journals such as RSE, ES&T, JGR, ACP, and TGRS, in which 5 papers have been selected as ESI Hot (Top < 0.1%) papers, 12 papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited (Top < 1%) papers, and 5 papers have been cited more than 100 times. My total citation is more than 2400 times (Google) with an H-index of 28. I was selected in Stanford University List of World’s Top 2% Scientists. I have generated the high-resolution and high-quality datasets of ground-level air pollutants in China (i.e., ChinaHighAirPollutants, CHAP), which have been widely used.
