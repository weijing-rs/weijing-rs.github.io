Biography

I am mainly engaged in quantitative remote sensing and atmospheric environment. Up to now, I have published more than 70 academic papers in leading journals such as RSE, ES&T, TGRS, ACP, JGR and AE, which have been cited by more than 900 times (Google). H-index is 17. Among them, 3 papers have been selected as ESI Hot (Top < 0.1%) papers, and 5 papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited (Top < 1%) papers, and 1 paper has been selected as JGR highlight paper. I have won the “Li Xiaowen” Excellent Youth Award for Remote Sensing Science, and “Gao Tingyao” Outstanding Youth Award for Environmental Protection.
