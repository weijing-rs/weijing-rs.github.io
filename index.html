Biography

I am mainly engaged in quantitative remote sensing and atmospheric environment. Up to now, I have published more than 80 academic papers (including 28 first/corresponding-author SCI papers) in leading journals such as RSE, ES&T, TGRS, ACP, and JGR, which have been cited more than 1200 times (Google). H-index is 20. Among them, 3 papers have been selected as ESI Hot (Top < 0.1%) papers, and 6 papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited (Top < 1%) papers, and 1 paper has been selected as JGR highlight paper. I have won the “Li Xiaowen” Excellent Youth Award for Remote Sensing Science, and “Gao Tingyao” Outstanding Youth Award for Environmental Protection. I am a Guest Editor of the Remote Sensing and a Topic Editor of the Big Earth Data.
