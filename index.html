Biography

I am focusing on studying atmospheric aerosols, clouds, particulate matters, and trace gases. Up to now, I have authored over 100 papers (including 37 first- or corresponding-author SCI papers) in leading journals such as RSE, ES&T, ACP, and JGR, which have been cited more than 1700 times (Google). H-index is 24, and i10-index is 44. Among them, 5 papers have been selected as ESI Hot (Top < 0.1%) papers, and 9 papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited (Top < 1%) papers. I have generated the high-resolution and high-quality datasets of ground-level air pollutants in China (i.e., ChinaHighAirPollutants, CHAP), which has been widely used.
